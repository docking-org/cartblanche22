CURL commands for searching single ZincID

to get .json file: https://cartblanche22.docking.org/search.json?zinc_id=ZINCms000002NiP3

to get .csv file: https://cartblanche22.docking.org/search.csv?zinc_id=ZINCms000002NiP3

CURL commands for searching multiple ZincIDs

to get .txt file: curl https://cartblanche22.docking.org/substances.txt -F zinc_id-in=@test.txt -F output_fields='smiles,sub_id'

to get .json file: curl https://cartblanche22.docking.org/substances.json -F zinc_id-in=@test.txt

to get .csv file: curl https://cartblanche22.docking.org/substances.csv -F zinc_id-in=@test.txt
